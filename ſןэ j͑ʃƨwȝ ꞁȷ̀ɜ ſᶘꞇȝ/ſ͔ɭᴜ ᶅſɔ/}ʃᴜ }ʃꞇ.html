Pronouns

Iikrhian is a register language as shown below. Despite having many pronouns, Iikrhian is a pronoun drop language. Usually halfway within a conversation or in public areas, pronouns and names are dropped and are based on context.

The register system in Iikrhian arose from avoidance taboo (qanasaf) via tsacek where I would use different sets of vocabulary for different people.

Pronouns for non-humans

This is used for games and shows and books where you are not human.

Use pronoun + -j͑ʃᴜꞇ + name of thing.

For third person, just use the name of the thing like mormal.

Custom

Adding to the complexity, variants of names can be used in place of the 1st person pronoun due to taboo. These will differ from person.
