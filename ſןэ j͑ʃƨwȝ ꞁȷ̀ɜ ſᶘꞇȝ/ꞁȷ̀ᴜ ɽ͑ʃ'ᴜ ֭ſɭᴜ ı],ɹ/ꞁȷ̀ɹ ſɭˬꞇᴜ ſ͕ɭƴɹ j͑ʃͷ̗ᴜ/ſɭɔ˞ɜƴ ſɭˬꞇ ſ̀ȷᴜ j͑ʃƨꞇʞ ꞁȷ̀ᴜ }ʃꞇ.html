Ksorhkrhitla (After the Ice Age)

Map

Center Region

Many animals started living on the ground. Many species also became extinct. On the left is a descendant of the mcephatsoupra called the tsoupralsootsmii. Its bottom tentacle is used to eat ground plants. Now, the shell is thinner and resembles a spine. Its ear became larger and it developed eyelids. On the right is a descendant of the mcephatsouce called the tsoucerhxasuhash. its eyes moved up to allow it to see more. Now its ears are above its head so that it is more protected. The middle legs have become smaller so that it can run faster and then repurposed to hold newborns. In the middle is a type of tpiinii called niipoowtsoctiik. All tpiiniipoo cannot fly and instead jump. Wings are still used although for stabilization and also are colorful for mates in many species. Also, the lip has become a beak so that it can cut leaves.
