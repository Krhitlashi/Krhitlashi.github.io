Isawat

Phonology

Grammar

OSV
Occasionally, SVO.

Adjective after nouns.

Verbs

Verbs are conjugated with prefixes.

e- infinitive
i- 1st
ti- 2nd
sa- 3rd
mi- 1pl exclusive
hi- 1pl inclusive
mati- 2pl
masa- 3pl

wo- future tense
ma- past tense

The plural is m-/ma-.

makʷemat wotimosu yis ena takʷ
ma-kʷemat wo-ti-mosu yis ena takʷ
PL-wheel FT-2s-bring in night this
you'll bring the wheels tonight

"if" is always after the word. Also negatives although they are duplicated.

hoɻien emas al taem uel
flower to.hold if time difficult
hold the flower during hard times

moisaenan na masaɻope na
PL-pineapple NEG 3PL-fly-FACT NEG
pineapples don't fly

Evidential Affixes

-pe factual
-kʷe witnessed
-ɻene reported

Emphasis can be done by using -pe in more places.

moisaenan nape masaɻope na
PL-pineapple NEG-FACT 3PL-fly-FACT NEG
pineapples don't fly

moisaenanpe masaɻope
PL-pineapple-FACT 3PL-fly-FACT
pineapples do fly!

Repetition exists.
